Where is profile.jkr

Windows

  1. In the File Explorer window, click the address bar.
  2. Type %AppData%/Balatro. Press [Enter].
  3. Select the profile.jkr file in the folder of the profile that you want to see.

macOS

  1. In the Finder window, press [Cmd]+[Shift]+[G].
  2. Type ~/Library/Application Support/Balatro, then press [return].
  3. Select the profile.jkr file in the folder of the profile that you want to see.

SteamOS

  1. Go to ~/.local/share/Steam/steamapps/compatdata/2379780/pfx/drive_c/users/steamuser/AppData/Roaming/Balatro.
  2. Select the profile.jkr file in the folder of the profile that you want to see.

Android

  1. Go to Google Takeout.
  2. Deselect all. Then select only "Google Play Games Services".
  3. Scroll down. Find "Next Step" at the bottom of the page and click.
  4. Send download link via email as a .zip and then click "Create Export".
  5. Wait for the file to be sent to you. This usually takes around 10-20 minutes.
  6. Unzip the Takeout .zip file. In the Takeout folder, go to Takeout > Google Play Games Services > Games > Balatro > Saved Games.
  7. Choose the #-profile.jkr that you want to see the data for. (For your first profile, choose 1-profile.jkr.)
  8. You may upload the data.bin file directly without extracting the profile.jkr file.
Ranks

Ranks are a fun, unofficial way to see your deck results at a glance. You can rank up by playing more games or improving your win percent.

Bronze
Play at least 1 game.
Silver
Play at least 10 games and win at least 25% of them.
Gold
Play at least 25 games and win at least 50% of them.
Diamond
Play at least 50 games and win at least 75% of them.
